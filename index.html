About Project

AI-enhanced recycling for smarter waste segregation

What it does

The system uses a TensorFlow.js model exported from Google Teachable Machine to classify waste from images in the browser.

Why it matters

Proper segregation increases recycling rates and reduces contamination in recycling streams, improving efficiency across collection and processing.

How AI helps

AI removes the guesswork by identifying material types instantly and helps users make better recycling decisions on the fly.

Environmental benefits

Faster sorting reduces landfill waste, conserves resources, and supports a circular economy through smarter material reuse.

How It Works

From image to insight in four easy steps

Upload or webcam

Choose a waste image or activate your camera to provide a live sample.

TensorFlow.js model

The browser loads the trained model and analyzes the uploaded frame instantly.

AI identifies

The model classifies the waste into plastic, paper, metal, or glass.

Result displayed

The UI presents the detected category with confidence and recycling guidance.
